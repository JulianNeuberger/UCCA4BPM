In the case of catalogue parts, in accordance with the frame conditions, a standard offer is submitted and the offer process is concluded.
In the case of installations, first of all the feasibility is clarified and then a fundamental decision made as to whether an offer is to be submitted.
Depending upon the complexity of the offer, the offer is drawn up by an interdisciplinary project team comprising all the technical departments involved (e.g. customer centre, production, purchasing and quality ...) or it is attended to by the customer centre.
