An order to cash process starts when a customer order is received.
After the order has been processed, it is send to the warehouse.
The warehouse employees determine the parts required to produce the requested items.
If any parts are not available in the warehouse, these are ordered.
Once all necessary parts are available, production will start.
After production, quality checks will be performed.
If the production process has finished, products will be packaged and labeled, before returning to the warehouse.
Finally, sales will be informed about the completed production and the product can be sold to the customer.
